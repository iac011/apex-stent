The APEX-STENT project aims to conduct a comprehensive review of traditional stents explanted from patients and to investigate next-generation alloys for permanent and biodegradable uses. Distinguished by our comprehensive collection of stent samples from patients, our pioneering project also researches novel alloys in animals. We have identified Ti and Zr alloys as promising candidates due to their excellent biocompatibility and corrosion resistance. Furthermore, our ongoing research on biodegradable alloys shows considerable potential for their use in stent applications.

Our goal is to create a publicly accessible and high-impact stent database, supported by detailed analyses, facilitating the optimal selection of stent materials. The development of this database and its automated imaging will leverage CRESt technology (https://youtu.be/POPPVtGueb0), developed by the MIT team in collaboration as an external partner of the APEX project. In the custom CRESt-APEX system, a machine learning engine will be developed to help analyze different types of images of the same sample, obtained by electron microscopy, histological staining, endovascular imaging, and multiphoton microscopy. Powered by machine learning, the database promises to transform stent research by providing public access to vast, high-quality raw data and advanced analytical tools. 

This pioneering approach can identify links between specific stent-related complications and patient or stent characteristics, predict the performance of new materials in human patients based on animal trial data, and simulate complication progression in individual patients. The ultimate goal of APEX-STENT is to carefully evaluate current stents explanted from patients and compare them to next-generation alloys (Ti, Zr, Fe, Zn, Mg) for permanent and biodegradable stents. With our comprehensive inventory of stent samples from patients and animals, and the unparalleled expertise of our consortium, we are uniquely positioned to build a comprehensive and impactful stent database for public use, leveraging Artificial Intelligence to achieve our goals.
