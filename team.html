Meet the Team

Our team is united by a shared commitment to advancing biodegradable coronary stent technologies, combining expertise in materials science, biomedical engineering, and clinical translation to achieve real-world impact.

Leadership

Dr. Fan SUN – Project Leader, PSL University (Chimie ParisTech). Dr. SUN leads the APEX Stent project with a multidisciplinary background in materials science and electron microscopy.

The team is co-organized by Dr. Yun LUO, a senior researcher at Paris Cité University. She holds the BIORESORB Project which ANR funds.

Dr. Guanyu CAI is a postdoctoral researcher at PSL University, and he is responsible for creating the database and AI assistant. Dr. Cai's vision and interdisciplinary background drive the innovation and coordination at the heart of the APEX Stent project.

Dr. Graciela Pavon-Djavid is a senior engineer at Paris Sorbonne Nord University, responsible for the histological preparation and research.

Team Culture

We foster a collaborative, research-driven culture that values open communication, innovation, and a strong sense of purpose toward solving critical healthcare challenges.
